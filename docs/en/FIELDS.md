# Available Variables

```xml


<g:id>: Unique product identifier.
<title>: Product title.
<description>: Product description.
<link>: Product page URL.
<g:image_link>: Main image URL of the product.
<g:additional_image_link>: Additional product images.
<g:availability>: Product availability.
<g:price>: Product price.
<g:sale_price>: Product sale price.
<g:sale_price_effective_date>: Sale price effective dates.
<g:google_product_category>: Google's product category.
<g:product_type>: Your product category.
<g:brand>: Product brand.
<g:gtin>: Global Trade Item Number.
<g:mpn>: Manufacturer Part Number.
<g:identifier_exists>: Identifier existence flag.
<g:condition>: Product condition.
<g:adult>: Adult product flag.
<g:multipack>: Multipack indicator.
<g:is_bundle>: Bundle indicator.
<g:energy_efficiency_class>: Energy efficiency class.
<g:age_group>: Target age group.
<g:color>: Product color.
<g:gender>: Target gender.
<g:material>: Product material.
<g:pattern>: Product pattern.
<g:size>: Product size.
<g:size_type>: Type of product size.
<g:size_system>: Sizing system.
<g:item_group_id>: Item group identifier.
<g:adwords_redirect>: AdWords redirect URL.
<g:custom_label_0> through <g:custom_label_4>: Custom labels.
<g:promotion_id>: Promotion ID.
<g:shipping>: Shipping details.
<g:shipping_weight>: Shipping weight.
<g:shipping_length>, <g:shipping_width>, <g:shipping_height>: Shipping dimensions.
<g:tax>: Tax information.
<g:unit_pricing_measure>: Unit pricing measure.
<g:unit_pricing_base_measure>: Base unit for pricing measure.
<g:installment>: Installment details.
<g:loyalty_points>: Loyalty points.
<g:excluded_destination>: Excluded destinations.
<g:included_destination>: Included destinations.
<g:shopping_ads_excluded_country>: Excluded countries for Shopping ads.
```
